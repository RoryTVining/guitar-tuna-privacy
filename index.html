# Privacy Policy for Guitar Tuna **Last updated: January 01, 2026** Guitar Tuna is a free educational app for tuning guitars and exploring sound frequencies and resonance. ### Data Collection Guitar Tuna **does not collect, store, or share any personal data**. - The app requests microphone permission **only** to analyze live audio for real-time tuning and resonance detection. - No audio is recorded, saved, or transmitted. - No user information (name, email, location, device ID, etc.) is collected. - No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used. ### Permissions Explained - **Microphone**: Used temporarily and only while the app is active to detect pitch and frequencies. Audio processing happens entirely on your device. ### Third Parties This app does not use any third-party services, libraries, or SDKs that collect data. ### Children's Privacy Guitar Tuna is safe for all ages, including children under 13.
